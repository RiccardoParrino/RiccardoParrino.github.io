Introduction to Marketing for Software Developer

#marketing #software #developer #advice

Introduction

Marketing operations can enhance your visibility through the other developer, standing out from the crowd. In this little article I'll try to explain you what are the main operations that you can do as a software developer for reach a better visibility. You can find most of the informations and skills of this post explained in much more detail in the book recommended in the bibliography. In particular, the third one Sonmez is the book that introduce me to the topic. There you can find the main marketing ops that use software developer, like blogging, writing book and uploading video tutorial. In the other two books, you can find an introduction to all of the general concepts in marketing, like marketing strategy, delivering values through service or product and other marketing skills.

Prerequisites

There're no particular prerequisites needed. But, for sure you will need some speaking and writing skills (and maybe also thinking skills).

Introduction to Marketing what is marketing

Marketing is the art of making money satisfying the customer needs.

customer behavior

Market research is the operation to gain insight and information on customer need in some particular market. This can be done by using interviews, focus groups or by secondary research.

Segmentation is the act of clustering or indentify groups in a certain market. Targeting is the operation of choosing one or more segment of the market, to make business profitable and actionable. Positioning is the act of differentiating a product from his competitors.

A product is anything that can be exchanged by money. There is a distinction between product and services that is the property of tangibility. The product is more tangible than the service, but there is no clear distinction between the former and the latter, because there is always a part of untangible service in process (like the Apple buyer experience or the Ferrari process of product customization) and there is always a part of tangible product in a service (like in psychoterapy, maybe the most untagible process, the tangible part of the service is in fact the setting in which it is delivered).

For every product you can delivery there is always a distinction between the core functionality of the product, the actual product and the external product. For every of this aspect of the product, you can make a difference from the competitions.

With branding it is mean the identification of the product. It is what you feel in your gut when you hear the name of a particular product or company. In a more scientifically way, suppose of doing a test (called the blind test) like this: let's have two product, that are technically identical and also from an exterior perspective, but they come from different company, let's A and B: if you choose A, instead of B, that's where branding come out.

When you deliver a service there are always cue and fact that differentiate you from the competition.

  • Develope a offer
  • State the offering
  • Develope a brand
  • Develope an inbound strategy
  • Develope an outbound strategy
  • Identify a niche (Market Segmentation and Targeting)
  • Differentiate you offer from the other (Market positioning)
  • State your pricing strategy
